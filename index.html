Visit Herceg Novi

Enjoy the mountains and the sea in this beautifully nested slice of heaven.

Top Three Activities To Do in Herceg Novi

Boat Trip to the Blue Cave

Take a charter boat out for a sight-seeing trip on the water. Visit our famous and beautiful blue caves, where you can swim!

Mimosa Festival

Join us in this annual celebration of the season change with food, live music, and day long avtivities.

Hike Orjen

Nested right above the city, Orjen has the most beautiful view of the whole Bay of Kotor. Get there early in the morning for some beautiful sunrise views.
